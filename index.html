A bit about me...

Greetings weary internet traveler! My name is Greg Lawrence, and as you've probably already figured out I am among many other things, a web developer.

Apart from web development I enjoy many other activities such as
hiking, mountain biking, cars, music production, and plenty more.

The very astute of you may have also noticed my favorite color happens to be blue.

I have always loved learning new skills of all types, but that has of course also spilled over into my programming career as well.

Whether it be learning simple concepts such as new CSS styling techniques, learning JavaScript libraries such as React.js and Three.js, or even learning new Vim keybindings I have always sought out the unique and interesting in life, allowing me to in turn create equally unique websites and web applications.
