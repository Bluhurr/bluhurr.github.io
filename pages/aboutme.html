Hello there! My name is Greg and I am a Boston area local web designer & developer. With a BS in Computer Science, I've certainly dabbled in all types of programming, but have always been drawn back to the web. I specifically love being able to bring in my creativity and create online experiences that are unique and create intrigue for users.

Apart from trying to create novel 3D experiences and gamified web interactions, I also find plenty of other hobbies and interests to fill my time. I especially enjoy the gym/powerlifting, hiking in the New Hampshire White Mountains, pretty much all things computers (of course including my fair share of video games), and even music production.

I always try to bring a bit (or a lot) of fun and playfulness into my work, because if we can't find fun in the things we do, then why are we doing them in the first place?
