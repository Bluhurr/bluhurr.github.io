Here you'll find the part where I talk about myself and my random interests.

Apart from web development I've always enjoyed a wide array of different activities and interests, such as coffee, hiking, skateboarding, old and unique cars, music production, and pretty much any novel and interesting hobby I can get my hands on.

In general I have always enjoyed working with computers, especially toying with different Linux distros Recently, my main focus has been learning Vim, a powerful and highly customizable terminal based text editor. As with most Vim enthusiasts, I get excited in the discovery of new Vim commands that reduce my keystrokes by a whopping 2 or 3!

In web development I have always thrived in situations where I get to apply my creativity and love for creating visually pleasing and user friendly designs.
